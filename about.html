About Envy Wheel Hubs

Envy by Metrics is a UK-based performance wheel hub brand focused on engineering precision, durability, and sleek design for cycling enthusiasts.

Born from a passion for quality materials and high-spec CNC manufacturing, our hubs are built to handle whatever your ride throws at them — from muddy trails to urban streets.

Our Mission

At Envy, our mission is to push the limits of wheel hub performance through precision engineering and thoughtful design. We build components that riders can trust—strong, reliable, and made to perform wherever the ride takes them.

"Design with purpose. Engineer with obsession. Ride with confidence."

— The Envy Engineering Team

Why Choose Envy?

  • Lightweight aerospace-grade materials
  • Extensive field and fatigue testing
  • Customisable colours and axle options
  • Precision-sealed bearings
